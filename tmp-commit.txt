feat(R417+R419): focus validation board after successful generation

Generate already announced a summary via srAnnouncement and scrolled
the viewport, but focus stayed on the Generate button inside the
founder-brief aside, forcing keyboard/screen-reader users to tab
through the cloud history list before reaching the generated
hypotheses.

After the successful setState pass and isBriefOpen(false) we now queue
a requestAnimationFrame that looks up #validation-board (added in
R404 as the ValidationBoard root landmark), sets tabindex=-1, calls
focus() and smooth-scrolls to it so AT users begin navigation at the
top of the generated loop. Falls back to the existing #workspace-main
focus if the board is ever unmounted by a future refactor.
