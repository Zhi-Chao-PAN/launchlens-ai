feat(R432): aria-invalid surfaces over-long brief fields to assistive tech

Idea already had aria-invalid for too-short input, and all five brief
fields had soft colour-coded char counters, but audience / market /
constraints did not expose an invalid state programmatically when
they blew past their red thresholds. That meant screen readers saw
an ordinary text field even when the prompt was going to be bloated.

Added aria-invalid={<length over red threshold>} to audience (>400),
market (>200) and constraints (>500). aria-describedby already
points at the relevant count <p>, so AT now announces the field as
invalid and the count gives the reason. Idea keeps its existing
<20-chars invalid rule and now also contributes a uniform
experience across the whole brief.

R431 was verified to already be correct: the Generate button only
references founder-generate-reason while isGenerating is true, and
the element mounts under the same guard, so there is no stale id
after cancel.
